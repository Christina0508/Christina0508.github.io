​ Some people believe that young people who commit serious crimes should be punished in the same way as adults. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
Nowadays,there are some young people who commit crimes.There is a huge controversy on weather we should punish them in the same way as adults.From my perspective,I agree that we shouldn’t punish them in the same as adults.
​ Admitedly,punishing young people as the same as adults has positive effects because this can help them learn a lesson.As the way to punish adults is too serious, this can make them bear in mind.In this way, young children won’t make same mistakes again.As a result,they will learn and behave.
​ However,punishing them as adults will bring more problems than benefits.As young people’s mind and body haven’t developed maturely.So serious punishments can may produce negative effects to them, this may cause them to have psychological shadow.In this way,these experiences may influence their development.There are many felonies in the prison, so it will be easy for young people to recommit in the future.As a result,it will be difficult for young people to rehabilitate in the future.
Although there’re some positive sides for punishing young as adults, there will be more negative effects in fact.From my perspective,we shouldn’t treating young people who commit as adults.
