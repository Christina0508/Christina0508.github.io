Stage summary from 7.1 to 7.20

I would like to describe my life in these twenty days, the following summary includes three parts.

Firstly, I’d like to talk about my results in these days. First of all, in my IELTS study, I continued to do exercises three times a week. I thought this part was normal because I did these exercises as usual. I will have a real IELTS test next month, so I’m preparing for it. During these days, I haven’t found any efforts in my points, I find that I may feel tired because of the longtime exercises, so I have to adjust myself to a better condition. In this way, maybe I can get a better point next month. Secondly, in my school study, I have done some preview sections, I have previewed three chapters of Chemistry, one chapter of Physics and two chapters of Math (Pure1). I have also had two economic lessons and one easy- commentary about a piece of news. Then, I have updated my blog once a week. In addition, I also had three python lessons every week to continue my coding part.

Then, I’d like to talk about my plan of next twenty days. The most important thing is to pass my IELTS test, I hope this part can be finished soon. I don’t want to delay it for so long time. This will let me more tired and more difficult to pass it. I plan to memory more words to help me cope with speaking and wring part. Next, I plan to continue preview next term’s books. My plan is to preview two chapters of each subject and I think I have to do some exercises. During last twenty days, I haven’t done many exercises because I haven’t learned much about new book. However, I think it’s really an important part of my preview part. In addition, I think I will write another commentary about news next twenty days after taking some lessons. Exercises can help me to consolidate the knowledge, this will help me to do better next term. Then, I plan to continue updating my blog once a week. I will also continue to have python lessons every week. Then, I think I will have a new part next twenty days, I think I will have some activities to enrich myself.

Summer holiday is one essential part for one year. I have done much in these twenty days and I hope I can do better in next twenties. I think I can plan and control my time in more effective way because actually I have wasted much time in this twenty days.
