role model

I love football very much and my favorite football player is Lionel Messi.
People all think Messi is a football genius, he is a man who is blessed by God. But after he showed a talent for football as a child, doctors told him he had dwarfism and that his family would not be able to pay for his medical bills. No player is known to suffer from this kind of disease, and it seemed to tell Messi who is only 12, that his football career was over. Undaunted, he pursued his football dream until the world-famous Barcelona club spotted him and offered to pay his medical bills. I admired him for his courage to insist his dream and I thought his success not only because of his talent, but also because his hard work. When I felt afraid and wanted to give up something, I told myself to insist because the challenge I faced was smaller than Messi’s.
If I can meet him, the question I wanted to ask him about his plan, because he will attend the fifth world cup this year and I also want to know his future destination whether he will come back to Barcelona. Messi is a very gentle person, but at the same time very fragile, I hope he can become confident and strengthen his leadership, as the captain, he is important for the development of Argentina team.
